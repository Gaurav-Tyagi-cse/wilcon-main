T.D.E.M.S Pvt Ltd is a multidisciplinary company of the Engineering & Management sector in India, registered under corporate acts 2013 of India. The company is ISO: 9001-2015 & IAF certified & registered in small category of MSME. The company is established as a pioneer of Engineering & Management sector services in India & abroad also, providing Contracting and Consulting services to clients of different engineering & management sectors.
